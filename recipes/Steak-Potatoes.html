Steak & Potatoes!

Description-

Juicy and tender medium rare New York steak alonng with buttery and herbs golden brown potatoes!

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 inch New York steak
  • Baby Gold potatoes
  • Butter
  • Rosemary
  • Thyme
  • Garlic
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Season with salt only your steak and leave uncovered over night on the fridge.
  2. Take your dry brined steak out 30 minutes before cooking.
  3. On a hot cast iron skillet start by placing your salted steak, fat side first, to render fat.
  4. With the fat rendered, place the steak 4 minutes on each side, lightly pressing it to create a crust.
  5. 2 minutes before taking out your steak, throw in butter, garlic and thyme and base the steak with its juices.
  6. Cut your potatoes in half and drizzle with olive oil and salt placing it on an oven until golden brown and soft in the middle.
  7. Let steak rest for 10 minutes covered.
  8. Finish the potatoes with fine cut rosemary.
  9. Enjoy!
